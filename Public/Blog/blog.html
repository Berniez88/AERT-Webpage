Blog Page

This blog is to showcase examples and updates of the Geostationary Lightning Mappers (GLMs) on the GOES-East and -West satellites. It is maintained by scientists at the NOAA's Cooperative Institute for Satellite Earth System Studies (CISESS), located in the Discovery District at the University of Maryland, College Park. If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions about the CIMSS Satellite Blog, feel free to send us email.
